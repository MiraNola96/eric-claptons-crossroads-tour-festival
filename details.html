Event Details

Crypto.com Arena

The Crossroads Guitar Festival Village will be located on Peacock Place and Chick Hearn Court this Saturday and Sunday, September 23rd and 24th with live performances from 9:00am – 3:00pm each day. The Crossroads Guitar Festival Village is included in your ticket for the concert at Crypto.com Arena that evening and is not open to the general public and will include live music on stage at Peacock Place as well as partner activations, event merchandise tents and food and beverage on Chick Hearn Court.

All tickets sales for this event will be on Ticketmaster.com. We will not be selling tickets in person this weekend, all sales are online only!

If you have questions prior to the event, you may contact the Box Office directly at 213-742-7340 or you may email the box office at boxoffice@cryptoarena.com. If you purchased tickets from a third-party resale site, please reach out to them directly for assistance on your order.
